# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Panoptis?
Panoptis is an AI-powered chat assistant that can answer questions in multiple languages and provide information about our app.

### How do I start using Panoptis?
Simply type your message in the chat box and press enter or click the send button. Panoptis will respond instantly.

### What languages does Panoptis support?
Panoptis supports multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, and many more. Just chat in your preferred language.

## Technical Questions

### How does Panoptis work?
Panoptis uses Cohere's advanced AI models to understand your questions and provide helpful responses. It also has access to a knowledge base with specific information about our app.

### Is my conversation with Panoptis private?
Yes, your conversations with Panoptis are not stored permanently and are only used to provide you with the best responses.

### Can I integrate Panoptis with my own app?
Yes, we offer API access for enterprise customers. Please contact our sales team for more information.

## Billing Questions

### How much does Panoptis cost?
Panoptis offers a free tier with basic features and premium plans starting at $9.99/month. See our pricing page for more details.

### How do I upgrade my plan?
You can upgrade your plan by visiting your account settings and selecting the plan that best fits your needs.

### Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all new premium subscriptions.
